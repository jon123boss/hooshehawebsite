Key Requirements

  • Current Y10-Y12 students
  • Basic Chinese language skills required
  • Valid passport with 6+ months validity
  • Ability to work in team environments

Essential Information

📝 Visa Requirements

Families must ensure proper visa documentation. Allow 8-12 weeks for processing.

⚖️ Behavior Policy

Strict adherence to safety protocols. Serious misconduct may result in dismissal.

🛡️ Comprehensive Insurance

Full Allianz Travel Insurance included for all participants.

Participant Responsibilities

  • Create lesson slides for 2 classes
  • Develop interactive educational games
  • Supervise students throughout activities
  • Collaborate with teaching teams daily

Daily Teaching Format (Tentative)

🌞 Morning Sessions

2 head teachers conduct lessons while 2 assist students

🌆 Afternoon Sessions

Other 2 head teachers lead lessons while morning team assists students

🌙 Evening Prep

All teachers review progress and prepare next day's materials
